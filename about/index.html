Reporting and comment on the arts in Madison, Wisconsin.

Tone Madison covers music and culture in Madison, Wisconsin. Our journalism reflects a smart, curious, complex, and flawed city.

Tone Madison is wholly owned and operated by its editors and writers. No shilling, no fillerâ€”just our best efforts at providing stories, conversations, and viewpoints you can't get from any other local outlet.

We have some paid advertisements and sponsor shout-outs, but they'll be clearly delineated and not disguised as stories. Also, our readers support us with recurring donations through Patreon and one-time donations through Square Cash.

In addition to the stories we post throughout the week, we also produce a weekly podcast and frequently present curated shows and other events.

Contact us: editor@tonemadison.com

Interested in advertising? We have simple, affordably priced ad plans designed with Madison small business owners in mind. Reach scottgwrites@gmail.com for more information.
